Sequences have the following name structure: 
-The taxonomic level at Class level ('Nass' for Nassellaria and 'Spum' for Spumellaria),
-the primer name with which it was sequenced (S30, S69(f) and d1r), 
-the cell identifier (Mge17-81, Mge17-82, Vil325 and Vil496), 
-the replicate identifier and 
-the well position 
separated with '_':

class_primer_cell_replicate_well

i.e.; Nass_d1r_Vil325_BC02_A6, Spum_S30_Mge17-81_BC08_C12
